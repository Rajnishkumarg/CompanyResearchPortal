Company Overview

Founded in 1983, RACL Geartech Ltd. is a leading Indian manufacturer of high-precision automotive components, primarily transmission gears and shafts. The company has successfully transitioned from focusing on the domestic market to becoming a globally recognized supplier for premier OEMs. RACL specializes in complex, high-tolerance components for luxury motorcycles, all-terrain vehicles (ATVs), agricultural machinery, and the rapidly growing Electric Vehicle (EV) segment. Its reputation for quality and reliability has resulted in long-standing, sticky relationships with marquee clients like BMW Motorrad, Kubota, and ZF.
